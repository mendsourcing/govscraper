GovScraper Mailbox Connections FAQ
1. How many individual mailboxes can I connect?
Each GovScraper user can connect one individual mailbox to send emails directly from inside GovScraper using their own email address (Gmail, Outlook). We are currently working on ways to have multiple users on one GovScraper account.
2. Will emails sent be stored in GovScraper?
Yes. Emails sent from GovScraper are logged in your account activity feed so you can track vendor communication. They will also appear in your connected email provider's "Sent" folder, just like a normal email.
3. Does GovScraper read or synchronize my inbox?
No. GovScraper never reads or imports emails from your inbox. The connection is only used to send emails on your behalf and log them into GovScraper for tracking purposes.
4. Will GovScraper ever send emails without my knowledge?
No. Emails are only sent when you manually use the "Send Email" option. GovScraper will never send anything automatically without your action.
5. Can I tag or track emails sent from GovScraper?
Yes. Emails sent from GovScraper can be linked by looking at the RFQ itself and seeing the vendor and "Email Sent" checkbox marked. You can review the email in your provider's Sent folder.
6. What if my email provider blocks the connection?
Some providers may require enabling SMTP access or creating an "App Password" for third-party connections. If you encounter issues, check your provider's security settings or reach out to us for step-by-step instructions.
Disconnect mailbox?
You'll no longer be able to send vendor emails until you reconnect. Your sent history is preserved.
