About SPS Technologies Leading manufacturer of aerospace and defense fasteners supplying DLA procurement contracts since 1956. We produce all standard hex bolts from 3/8" to 1-1/2" diameter in grades 2, 5, and 8, in both zinc-plated and stainless finishes. We maintain a full range of metric fasteners and can accommodate MIL-SPEC packaging requirements per MIL-STD-2073. AS9100 and ITAR registered.
